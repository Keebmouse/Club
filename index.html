Deadmau5: The World's Greatest Canadian

This week on the Alberta Mau5 Hau5 blog, we're talking about Deadmau5 for the first time ever on the internet! Most other Angelfire Sites haven't picked up on this well-established and well-known artist yet. Deadmau5 was born Joel Zimmerman in 1891 under the roaring waters of Niagara Falls! His determination prevented him from drowning under the torrential pressure of the waterfall. There were no survivors. He showed interest in music from the early age of 5, which is where the "5" in his stage name comes from. His passion for playing the recorder later spawned the now classic remix of Jack U's "Where Are You Now?" which topped Billboard's all time greatest memes and fostered a deep and long lasting friendship between himself, Skrillex, and Diplo.

Early on in his career he was known as the "Dead Mouse Guy," no one knows why, and you totally can't look it up for yourself. Once he discovered sidechain compression, his career exploded! He released the epic dubstep track "Faxing Berlin." The track used a sample of an actual fax machine that he was still using for some reason to promote his music.
